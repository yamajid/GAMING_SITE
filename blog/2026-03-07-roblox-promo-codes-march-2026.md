## Do These Codes Give Robux?

No. Roblox promo codes give **free avatar items**, not Robux. The items are cosmetic accessories you can equip on your avatar.

Some of these promotional items become rare over time as old codes expire — meaning players who grab them early sometimes end up with sought-after collector items.
