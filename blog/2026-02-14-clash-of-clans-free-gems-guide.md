Gems are the most valuable resource in Clash of Clans. Here's every legitimate way to earn them — ranked by how many gems you'll actually get.

<!-- truncate -->

## Method 1: Achievements (Best for New Players)

The achievement system gives gems for hitting milestones in gameplay. These are one-time rewards, but new players can earn **2,000–5,000 gems** just from playing normally in their first few months.

High-value achievements to prioritize:
- **Bigger & Better** (upgrade Town Hall) — up to 1,000 gems total across all tiers
- **Gold Grab / Elixir Escapade** (collect resources) — 1,000+ gems combined
- **War Hero** (win wars) — 1,000 gems for long-term players

See our [full achievements guide](/docs/clash-of-clans/achievements-gems-guide) for the complete list with exact gem values.

## Method 2: Gem Mine (Best Long-Term)

The Gem Mine on your Builder Base produces free gems passively, every single day. At max level (level 6), it generates ~8 gems/day = **240 gems/month forever**.

Upgrade it as soon as possible — the sooner it's maxed, the more lifetime gems you earn. See our [Gem Mine guide](/docs/clash-of-clans/gem-mine) for the full level-by-level production breakdown.

## Method 3: Google Opinion Rewards

Google Opinion Rewards is a survey app that pays in Google Play credits. Since CoC supports Google Play purchases, these credits convert directly to gems.

- Install from Google Play Store (Android only)
- Complete short surveys (30–60 seconds)
- Earn $0.10–$1.00 per survey
- Use credits to buy gem bundles

Average earning: $3–10/month = 300–1,000 gems per month, effectively free.

## Method 4: Clan Games and Events

Clan Games run regularly and include gem rewards in the event shop. Points are earned by completing in-game challenges, and gem rewards are available at various point thresholds.

The reward amount varies by season, but active players who complete Clan Games consistently earn an extra 50–200 gems per cycle.

## What Not to Spend Gems On

- **Speeding up long upgrades** — terrible gem-to-value ratio
- **Resource purchases** (gold/elixir) — always farm raids instead
- **Rushing** — patience is the most important resource in CoC

**Best gem investments:** Extra Builders (permanent progression multiplier) and the Gem Mine itself.
