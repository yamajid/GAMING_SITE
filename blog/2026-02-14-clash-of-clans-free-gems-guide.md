Gems are the most valuable resource in Clash of Clans. Here's every legitimate way to earn them — ranked by how many you'll actually get.

## Method 1: Achievements (Best for New Players)

The achievement system gives gems for hitting gameplay milestones. These are one-time rewards, but new players can earn **2,000–5,000 gems** just from playing normally in their first few months.

High-value achievements to prioritize:
- **Bigger & Better** (upgrade Town Hall) — up to 1,000 gems total across all tiers
- **Gold Grab / Elixir Escapade** (collect resources) — 1,000+ gems combined across tiers
- **War Hero** (win Clan Wars) — 1,000 gems for long-term players
- **Sweet Victory** (trophy milestones) — 450 gems across all tiers

The key insight: most of these complete naturally from normal gameplay. You don't need to grind specifically for them — just play the game and they happen automatically.

See our [full achievements guide](/docs/clash-of-clans/achievements-gems-guide) for the complete list with exact gem values for every tier.

## Method 2: Gem Mine (Best Long-Term — Set and Forget)

The Gem Mine on your Builder Base produces free gems passively every day. At max level (level 6), it generates **~8 gems/day = 240 gems/month forever** with zero effort.

**Why upgrading early matters:** If you max the Gem Mine at month 1 versus month 12, you earn an extra 2,880 gems (12 months × 240/month) just from the head start. The upgrade cost (~11,000 gems total across all levels) pays back within 4 years of passive production.

The most important habit: **collect your Gem Mine every 5–6 days**. The mine has a storage limit of 50 gems — once full, production stops until you collect. Players who forget to collect are essentially wasting free gems.

See our [complete Gem Mine guide](/docs/clash-of-clans/gem-mine) for the full level-by-level production and ROI breakdown.

## Method 3: Google Opinion Rewards (Android Only)

Google Opinion Rewards is a survey app by Google that pays in Google Play credits. Since Clash of Clans supports Google Play purchases, these credits convert directly to gem bundles.

**Setup (takes 5 minutes):**
1. Install Google Opinion Rewards from the Play Store
2. Complete the initial profile survey
3. Answer surveys as they appear (usually 1–3 per week)
4. Accumulate credits and use them to buy CoC gem bundles

**Expected earnings:** $3–10/month in Play credits depending on your location and survey frequency. At CoC's gem bundle prices, that translates to approximately **300–1,000 free gems per month**.

This is 100% legitimate, directly supported by Google, and requires no account risk whatsoever.

## Method 4: Clan Games

Clan Games run every month and include a gem reward tier in the event shop. By completing missions during the event window, you earn event points that unlock reward tiers.

**Key details:**
- Gems are available at specific point thresholds in the reward track
- Participating in a strong, active clan dramatically increases your points
- Active players in good clans consistently earn 50–200 gems per Clan Games cycle

**Finding a good clan:** Look for clans that are Level 5+ and consistently achieve max rewards in Clan Games. A quick search in the game for "Clan Games max" will find many active options.

## Method 5: Builder Base Versus Battles

Winning Versus Battles in the Builder Base earns Builder Gold and Builder Elixir — and occasionally small gem drops. This isn't a primary gem source, but active Builder Base players pick up 10–30 extra gems per month through this channel.

## What to Spend Free Gems On

Since patience is key in CoC, spend gems only on permanent improvements:

**Best investments:**
- Extra Builder huts — each additional builder permanently accelerates your entire progression
- Upgrading the Gem Mine to max level
- Builder Potions during Clan Games (high-value temporary speed boost)

**Avoid:**
- Rushing long upgrades (poor value per gem)
- Buying resources — farm raids instead
- Magic items that can be earned through events
