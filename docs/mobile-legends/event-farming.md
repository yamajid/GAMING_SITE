# Mobile Legends Event Farming Guide 2026

Events are the single best source of free diamonds and skins in Mobile Legends. A dedicated free-to-play player can earn **1–2 full Epic skins per month** purely through events — without spending a cent.

## Types of MLBB Events and What They Give

### 1. Login Events (Daily)
**Reward: 10–50 diamonds, hero trial cards, skin fragments**

Every season has a login calendar. Simply opening the game each day claims rewards. Never miss daily login — it's the easiest free diamonds in the game.

**Strategy:** Set a daily alarm. Even 30 seconds of login farming adds up to 300–1,500 diamonds over a month.

### 2. Limited-Time Story Events
**Reward: Skin fragments, epic skin selectors, avatar borders**

These are the big events — usually 2–4 weeks long — tied to game lore or collaborations. They require completing missions like:
- Playing X matches with specific heroes
- Achieving certain ranks in matches
- Completing story chapters

**Strategy:** Focus on missions that count your natural gameplay. Don't grind extra matches — just make sure event missions are always active when you play.

### 3. Starlight Member Events
**Reward: Exclusive skins, emblems, chest discounts**

Starlight membership (~$2.99/month) is the best value purchase in MLBB for players who want cosmetics. Each month includes an exclusive skin that can't be bought any other way, plus bonus event participation.

If you buy nothing else, Starlight is worth it.

### 4. Ranked Season Events
**Reward: Season-exclusive skins, avatar borders, recall effects**

Hitting certain ranked tiers (Epic, Legend, Mythic) unlocks cosmetic rewards each season. These are completely free — just play ranked games.

**Target ranks for free rewards:**
- Elite: Basic avatar border
- Epic: Exclusive hero skin (changes each season)
- Legend+: Enhanced skin + effects

### 5. Watch & Win Events
**Reward: 10–100 diamonds per event**

MLBB runs "watch official YouTube/Facebook streams for rewards" events several times a year. Link your account and watch for the duration specified.

### 6. Survey & Feedback Events
**Reward: 20–50 diamonds**

Moonton occasionally gives diamonds for completing in-game surveys about features. Accept all survey invitations.

## Monthly Event Farming Calendar

Here's a realistic breakdown of what a dedicated free-to-play player earns per month:

| Source | Diamonds/Month |
|--------|---------------|
| Daily login calendar | 100–300 |
| Story event participation | 200–500 |
| Ranked season rewards | 0–200 (seasonal) |
| Watch & Win events | 50–100 |
| Redeem codes | 50–150 |
| **Total** | **400–1,250/month** |

An Epic skin costs approximately 2,000 diamonds. At this rate, you can save for one in 2–4 months of consistent farming.

## Pro Tips for Maximum Event Diamonds

1. **Complete the event battlepass early** — Event passes often give bonus diamonds when completed before the event ends
2. **Don't spend on spins** — Gacha spins have terrible odds. Diamonds earned for free should be saved for direct skin purchases
3. **Join a squad/guild** — Guild events give bonus event currency
4. **Check the shop weekly** — Event shops rotate discounted skins that can be bought with event tokens, not diamonds

## Events to Avoid Spending On

These event types look rewarding but aren't worth spending diamonds:
- **Lucky spin events** — Expected cost to complete is 3,000–10,000 diamonds
- **Limited "pity" events** — Pity systems still cost hundreds of dollars to trigger
- **Collection events** — Usually require 80%+ of items to be purchased
