# Clash of Clans Gem Mine Guide 2026

The Gem Mine is one of the best long-term investments in Clash of Clans — it produces free gems passively, forever, once built. Here's everything you need to know.

## What Is the Gem Mine?

The Gem Mine is a Builder Base structure that produces gems at a slow but steady rate. Unlike regular resources, gems produced here don't require you to raid anyone — they just accumulate over time up to a storage limit.

**Location:** Builder Base (the island you access via the boat)

## Gem Mine Production by Level

| Level | Gems/Day | Gems/Month | Cost to Upgrade | Time to Upgrade |
|-------|---------|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| 1 | 4 | 120 | Free (starting) | — |
| 2 | 4.8 | 144 | 500 gems | 1 day |
| 3 | 5.6 | 168 | 1,000 gems | 2 days |
| 4 | 6.4 | 192 | 2,000 gems | 4 days |
| 5 | 7.2 | 216 | 3,000 gems | 6 days |
| 6 | 8 | 240 | 4,500 gems | 8 days |

**Max production: ~240 gems/month (8/day) at level 6**

## Is Upgrading the Gem Mine Worth It?

**Yes — overwhelmingly yes.** Here's the math:

Upgrading from level 1 to max (level 6) costs a total of **~11,000 gems**. The max mine produces 240 gems/month. That means the upgrade cost pays for itself in approximately **46 months (3.8 years)** — and then produces gems forever after.

But there's a better way to think about it: every gem you spend upgrading the mine was already "free" (earned from achievements or previous mine output). You're essentially reinvesting free gems to create a permanent gem income stream.

**Bottom line:** Upgrade the Gem Mine as early as possible. The sooner you max it, the more free gems you earn over your Clash of Clans lifetime.

## How to Collect Gems from the Mine

1. Open the game on your Builder Base
2. Tap the Gem Mine
3. Tap the gem icon to collect

The mine stores up to 50 gems before it's full. **Collect at least every 6 days** to avoid wasting production.

:::tip Set a reminder
The most common mistake is forgetting to collect. Set a weekly phone reminder to visit your Builder Base and collect gems.
:::

## Gem Mine vs. Other Free Gem Sources

| Source | Gems | One-Time or Ongoing |
|--------|------|---------------------|
| Gem Mine (max) | 240/month | Ongoing forever |
| Achievements | 500–3,000 total | One-time |
| Promo codes | 0–100 | Rare/occasional |
| In-game events | 50–200 | Seasonal |

The Gem Mine is the only **ongoing, reliable, passive** source of free gems in the game.

## What Should I Spend Gem Mine Gems On?

Since these gems are completely free, spend them strategically:

✅ **Good uses:**
- Upgrading the Gem Mine itself (level it up ASAP)
- Finishing Builder upgrades with a few hours left (saves time efficiently)
- Buying a Builder Potion during Clan Games for bonus rewards

❌ **Avoid:**
- Speeding up long upgrades (terrible value — costs hundreds of gems for a few days)
- Shop purchases (gem-to-resource conversions are terrible value)
- Impatience spending (never spend 200+ gems just because you're eager to progress)
