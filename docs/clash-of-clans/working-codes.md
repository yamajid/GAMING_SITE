## Why CoC Codes Are Rare

Supercell's philosophy is different from other games — they rarely use promo codes as a marketing tool. Instead, they focus on:

- **In-game events** with free rewards
- **Seasonal content** with free passes
- **Achievement systems** that permanently reward gems
- **Google Play / App Store rewards** through surveys

The most reliable free gems sources aren't codes at all — they're the [Achievement system](/docs/clash-of-clans/achievements-gems-guide) and the [Gem Mine](/docs/clash-of-clans/gem-mine).

## Where Legitimate Codes Come From

When CoC codes do exist, they come exclusively from:

1. **Clash of Clans official social media** (@ClashofClans on Twitter/X, YouTube)
2. **Clash Worlds / ESL events** — Esports partnerships occasionally include fan codes
3. **Content creator partnerships** — Major CoC YouTubers sometimes receive codes to share
4. **Supercell Store** — The official [Supercell Store](https://store.supercell.com) sometimes bundles codes with purchases

**Never trust codes from:** fan sites, Discord servers, Reddit posts without official source links, or any site asking for your Clash account login.

## Why You Shouldn't Rely on Codes for Gems

The Clash of Clans code system is fundamentally different from other games. Codes are rare and unpredictable — building a gem strategy around them will leave you disappointed.

The two most reliable gem sources that don't depend on codes:

**The Gem Mine** produces gems passively every single day. At max level it generates ~240 gems/month with zero effort. Upgrade it as early as possible. See our [Gem Mine guide](/docs/clash-of-clans/gem-mine) for the full level-by-level analysis.

**Achievements** give large one-time gem rewards. First-time completions of major achievements can award 50–1,000 gems each. New players can earn 2,000–5,000+ gems just from playing normally. See our [Achievements guide](/docs/clash-of-clans/achievements-gems-guide) for the complete list.

## Google Opinion Rewards: A Legitimate Gem Source

Google Opinion Rewards is a survey app by Google that pays in Google Play credits. Since Clash of Clans supports Google Play purchases, you can convert survey answers into free gems.

**How it works:**
1. Install Google Opinion Rewards from the Play Store
2. Answer short surveys (30–60 seconds each) when they appear
3. Earn $0.10–$1.00 in Google Play credit per survey
4. Use that credit to buy Clash of Clans gem bundles

Heavy users earn $5–20/month in Play credits. That's 500–2,000 gems per month effectively for free — far more consistent than promo codes.

**Note:** Only available on Android. iOS users can use Apple Surveys or gift card promotions instead.

## How to Spend Gems Wisely

Since gems are scarce, spending them poorly is the biggest mistake new players make.

**Best gem investments:**
- Upgrading the Gem Mine (reinvest free gems to earn more gems)
- Extra Builder huts — each additional builder dramatically speeds up your entire progression
- Builder Potions during Clan Games (maximum value per gem)

**Worst gem investments:**
- Speeding up long upgrades (terrible value — you need hundreds of gems per day saved)
- Buying resources (gold/elixir) — farm raids instead
- Magic items that can be earned through events

Patience is the most important resource in Clash of Clans. Save gems for permanent improvements, not impatience.
