## Why CoC Codes Are Rare

Supercell's philosophy is different from other games — they rarely use promo codes as a marketing tool. Instead, they focus on:

- **In-game events** with free rewards
- **Seasonal content** with free passes
- **Achievement systems** that permanently reward gems
- **Google Play / App Store rewards** through surveys

The most reliable free gems sources aren't codes at all — they're the [Achievement system](/docs/clash-of-clans/achievements-gems-guide) and the [Gem Mine](/docs/clash-of-clans/gem-mine).

## Where Legitimate Codes Come From

When CoC codes do exist, they come exclusively from:

1. **Clash of Clans official social media** (@ClashofClans on Twitter/X, YouTube)
2. **Clash Worlds / ESL events** — Esports partnerships occasionally include fan codes
3. **Content creator partnerships** — Major CoC YouTubers sometimes receive codes to share
4. **Supercell Store** — The official [Supercell Store](https://store.supercell.com) sometimes bundles codes with purchases

**Never trust codes from:** fan sites, Discord servers, Reddit posts without official source links, or any site asking for your Clash account login.
