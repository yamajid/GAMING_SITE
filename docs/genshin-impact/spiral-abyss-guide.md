# Genshin Impact Spiral Abyss Guide 2026

The Spiral Abyss is the most rewarding source of free Primogems in Genshin Impact — up to 600 Primogems every two weeks for players who can clear floors 9–12. Here's everything you need to know.

## What Is the Spiral Abyss?

The Spiral Abyss is Genshin Impact's end-game combat challenge. It consists of 12 floors of increasingly difficult enemy rooms. You bring two teams of four characters (8 characters total) and must clear each chamber within a time limit.

Floors 1–8 are permanent and give one-time Primogem rewards. Floors 9–12 reset every two weeks and give repeatable Primogem rewards — these are what matter for ongoing income.

## Primogem Rewards by Floor

| Floors | 3-Star Requirement | Primogems |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| Floor 9 (all 3 chambers) | 9 stars | 150 |
| Floor 10 (all 3 chambers) | 9 stars | 150 |
| Floor 11 (all 3 chambers) | 9 stars | 150 |
| Floor 12 (all 3 chambers) | 9 stars | 150 |
| **Full clear (36 stars)** | **All chambers** | **600** |

**Per month:** 600 Primogems × 2 resets = **1,200 Primogems/month** = ~7.5 free wishes per month from Abyss alone.

Players who only clear floors 9–10 still earn 300 Primogems per reset (600/month), which is meaningful even without a full clear.

## Do You Need a Fully Built Roster?

No — but you need a solid foundation. Here's what's realistic for free-to-play players at different stages:

**Adventure Rank 45+ with 2 well-built teams:** Can typically clear floors 9–10 consistently, floors 11–12 with effort.

**New accounts (under 6 months):** Focus on floors 9–10 first. Don't force floor 12 before your characters are ready — it wastes time and energy.

**Key insight:** The Abyss rewards investment in a few well-built characters far more than spreading resources across many mediocre ones. Pick 2 teams of 4, focus all your artifacts and talents on those 8 characters.

## Team Building Fundamentals

The Abyss requires two full teams, which means 8 characters. The most common free-to-play approach:

**Team 1 — Main DPS team:** Your strongest character + supports
**Team 2 — Second DPS team:** A different element/style to handle whatever Team 1 struggles with

**Free characters that work well in Abyss:**
- Traveler (Anemo/Geo/Dendro) — versatile support in multiple elements
- Amber, Kaeya, Lisa — starter characters with niche uses
- 4-star characters from events — Genshin regularly gives strong 4-stars for free through events

## The Star Rating System

Each Abyss chamber awards 0–3 stars based on whether both halves complete within the time limit. To earn full Primogem rewards for a floor, you need all 9 stars across its three chambers.

**If you're struggling to 3-star:** Focus on artifact quality over character level. Properly-statted artifacts at decent levels often matter more than character level 90.

## Abyss Reset Schedule

The Spiral Abyss resets on the **1st and 16th of each month**. After reset:
- Enemy lineup changes
- Your star count resets to 0 for floors 9–12
- Floor 1–8 progress is permanent (one-time rewards already claimed)

Set a calendar reminder for the 1st and 16th. Missing a reset is missing 600 Primogems — almost 4 wishes gone forever.

## Tips for Maximizing Stars

**Check the current Abyss buffs** — Each cycle includes elemental buffs that boost certain team compositions. Build around the current buff when possible.

**Time both halves carefully** — You need both halves of each chamber to complete within the time limit for 3 stars. If one half is consistently failing the timer, address that team's damage output first.

**Use food and potions** — These are often forgotten but provide meaningful stat boosts. Stock up on ATK and CRIT food before Abyss attempts.

**Don't waste time on Floor 12 if not ready** — Floors 9–10 give 300 Primogems per reset. Clearing those consistently is far better than spending hours failing floor 12.
