# How to Get Free Primogems in Genshin Impact 2026

Primogems are Genshin Impact's premium currency — used to buy Intertwined Fates for character and weapon wishes. The good news: Genshin is one of the most generous gacha games for free-to-play players. Here's every legitimate source.

:::tip Quick numbers
160 Primogems = 1 Intertwined Fate (1 wish). Most players earn 30–100 wishes per version (6 weeks) through free sources alone.
:::

## Daily Sources (Recurring Every Day)

### Daily Commissions — 60 Primogems/day
The single most important daily habit. Complete all 4 daily commissions from the Adventurers' Guild each day and collect the bonus reward from Katheryne.

- **4 commissions completed:** 20 Primogems
- **Daily bonus reward from Katheryne:** 20 Primogems
- **Total per day:** 60 Primogems
- **Total per month:** ~1,800 Primogems (~11 wishes)

Missing daily commissions is the single biggest mistake free-to-play players make. Set a daily reminder.

### Resin and Domains — Variable
Using Original Resin on Ley Line Outcrops occasionally drops Primogems, but this is inconsistent and not a reliable primary source.

## Weekly Sources

### Spiral Abyss — Up to 600 Primogems/cycle
The Spiral Abyss resets every two weeks. Clearing floors 9–12 with 3 stars each rewards significant Primogems:

| Floors | Stars Required | Primogems |
|--------|---------------|-----------|
| Floor 9 | 9 stars | 150 |
| Floor 10 | 9 stars | 150 |
| Floor 11 | 9 stars | 150 |
| Floor 12 | 9 stars | 150 |

Full clear (36 stars) = 600 Primogems every two weeks = ~1,200/month. This requires strong characters but is completely free once you have them.

### Weekly Boss Rewards — 60 Primogems/week
Defeating weekly bosses (Stormterror, Lupus Boreas, etc.) for the first time each week drops Primogems. Cap is 60 per week from bosses.

## Version Events — 400–1,600 Primogems per version

Every Genshin Impact version (lasting ~6 weeks) includes multiple limited-time events with Primogem rewards. These are the biggest free Primogem sources outside the Abyss.

Typical version event rewards:
- Main story event: 400–600 Primogems
- Side events (2–3 per version): 200–400 Primogems each
- **Total per version: 1,000–1,600 Primogems**

**Critical tip:** Never skip a version's events. Missing one event can cost you 400–600 Primogems — the equivalent of 2–4 wishes gone forever.

## One-Time Sources (Exploration)

### New Area Exploration — 600–2,000 Primogems per region
Every time HoYoverse releases a new map region, chests and puzzles throughout the area reward Primogems. First-time exploration of a new nation can yield 2,000+ Primogems.

### Archon Quest and Story Quests — Variable
Main story quests reward Primogems on completion. Each major Archon Quest chapter typically gives 200–600 Primogems total.

### Achievement System — Ongoing
The achievement system (Wonders of the World and category achievements) rewards Primogems for hundreds of milestones. Many are completed naturally through play; others require specific challenges.

Unlock all categories and aim for 3-star ratings in everything — total achievement Primogems across the game exceed 5,000.

## Promo Codes — 60–300 Primogems per code

HoYoverse regularly releases redemption codes during livestreams and special events. These give 60–100 Primogems each plus other materials.

**How to redeem:** Go to [genshin.hoyoverse.com/en/gift](https://genshin.hoyoverse.com/en/gift) → log in → enter code.

**Where to find codes:**
- Official HoYoverse Twitch/YouTube livestreams (every version preview)
- @GenshinImpact on Twitter/X
- r/Genshin_Impact — community posts codes within minutes

| Source | Monthly Primogems | Wishes Equivalent |
|--------|------------------|------------------|
| Daily commissions | ~1,800 | ~11 |
| Spiral Abyss (full clear) | ~1,200 | ~7 |
| Weekly bosses | ~240 | ~1.5 |
| Version events (avg) | ~1,100 | ~7 |
| **Total (active player)** | **~4,340** | **~27 wishes** |

A fully active free-to-play player earns roughly 27 wishes per month — enough to guarantee a featured 5-star character approximately every 2–3 months with smart pity management.

## Pity System: Making Every Wish Count

Understanding pity is essential for free-to-play players:

- **Soft pity:** Starts at wish 74 on character banners — 5-star rates begin increasing dramatically
- **Hard pity:** Guaranteed 5-star at wish 90
- **50/50 system:** First 5-star on a limited banner has 50% chance of being the featured character; if you lose, next 5-star is guaranteed featured

**Strategy:** Save wishes until you reach at least 75 pulls on a banner you want. Don't wish on banners you're not committed to — you risk bad pity and wasted currency.

## What NOT to Waste Primogems On

- **Resin refreshes** — Terrible value for free-to-play players
- **Buying Mora or EXP books** — Farm these through resin naturally
- **Welkin Moon** — This IS good value (~90 Primogems/day for $5/month), but it requires real money
