## When Are New Codes Released?

HoYoverse releases codes in a predictable pattern:

**Version preview livestreams** — Every ~6 weeks, HoYoverse hosts a livestream revealing the next version. Three codes worth 60–100 Primogems each are always shared. These are the most reliable source of free Primogems.

**Special events** — Genshin's anniversary, HoYoFest, and other milestone events come with dedicated codes.

**Collaborations** — When Genshin partners with brands or other games, promo codes are sometimes released.

**How to never miss a code:**
1. Watch or catch up on every version preview stream
2. Follow @GenshinImpact on Twitter/X
3. Bookmark this page — updated after every livestream

## What Codes Give You

Unlike some games, Genshin codes always give **real, useful rewards**:

- **Primogems** — directly used for wishes (60–100 per code)
- **Hero's Wit** — character EXP books
- **Mystic Enhancement Ore** — weapon EXP material
- **Mora** — gold currency

Three livestream codes typically give 300 Primogems total — equivalent to almost 2 full wishes per version, just for watching a 30-minute stream.
