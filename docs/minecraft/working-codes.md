## Where Minecraft Codes Come From

**Retail partnerships** — Physical Minecraft merchandise, game cards, and product bundles often include redemption codes for Marketplace content or Minecoins.

**Official promotions** — Mojang occasionally offers codes through official Minecraft social media channels for anniversaries or special events.

**Microsoft promotions** — Microsoft periodically offers Minecraft content through Xbox promotions, Game Pass perks, or Microsoft 365 bundles.

**Creator partnerships** — Minecraft content creators sometimes receive unique codes to share with their audiences.
