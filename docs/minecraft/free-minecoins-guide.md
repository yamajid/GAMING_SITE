# How to Get Free Minecoins in Minecraft 2026

Minecoins are Minecraft Bedrock Edition's currency — used to buy skins, texture packs, maps, and Marketplace content. Here are the legitimate ways to earn them for free.

:::info Java vs Bedrock
Minecoins only exist in Minecraft Bedrock Edition (Windows, mobile, console). Java Edition uses a separate skin/mod system that doesn't require Minecoins.
:::

## Method 1: Microsoft Rewards (Best Method)

Microsoft Rewards is the most reliable way to earn free Minecoins. Since Minecoins are sold through the Microsoft Store, you can use Microsoft Rewards points to buy them.

**How to earn Microsoft Rewards points:**
- Daily Bing searches (desktop + mobile) — ~15 points/day
- Microsoft Edge daily bonus — 15 points/day  
- Microsoft 365 and Xbox quests — 10–100 points each
- Monthly Microsoft Rewards challenges — up to 3,000 bonus points

**Conversion to Minecoins:**
1. Accumulate points (requires ~5,000–6,000 for a $5 Microsoft gift card)
2. Redeem points for Microsoft Store gift card
3. Use gift card to purchase Minecoins

**Expected timeline:** Most active users can earn a $5 Microsoft Store card every 3–6 weeks, which buys 500–750 Minecoins.

## Method 2: Google Opinion Rewards (Android/Mobile)

On Android, Google Opinion Rewards lets you earn Google Play credits through surveys. These can be used to buy Minecoins in the mobile version of Minecraft.

- Install Google Opinion Rewards from Play Store
- Complete short surveys (30–90 seconds each)
- Earn $0.10–$1.00 per survey
- Use credits to purchase Minecoins in-app

Active users in major markets earn $3–10/month in Play credits.

## Method 3: Xbox Game Pass Quests

If you have Xbox Game Pass, the Xbox app includes daily and weekly quests that award Microsoft Rewards points. Playing Minecraft through Game Pass while completing quests accelerates your Rewards earnings significantly.

Dedicated quest completers earn an additional 1,000–3,000 Rewards points per month — the equivalent of roughly $1–3 in Microsoft Store credit.

## Method 4: Promotional Events

Microsoft and Mojang occasionally run promotions that give free Minecoins or Marketplace content:

**Character Creator items** — Free character creator items are regularly added to Minecraft. While these aren't Minecoins, they reduce the need to spend coins on cosmetics.

**Free Marketplace content** — Mojang occasionally gives away free maps, skins, or texture packs directly through the Minecraft launcher.

**Anniversary promotions** — Minecraft's anniversary (November) typically includes special offers and occasionally free content.

## Method 5: Bing + Edge Combination Strategy

The fastest legitimate path to free Minecoins uses Microsoft's own ecosystem:

1. Set Bing as your default search engine
2. Do all daily searches in Microsoft Edge (PC + mobile app)
3. Complete all daily Microsoft Rewards quests (takes 5 minutes)
4. Complete weekly challenges and Xbox quests

**Results:** 300–600 Rewards points per day = roughly one $5 Microsoft Store card every 3–4 weeks = 500 Minecoins/month for free.

This is 100% legitimate, directly supported by Microsoft, and costs nothing but 5 minutes daily.

## How Many Minecoins Do You Need?

Understanding what things cost helps you prioritize:

| Item | Minecoin Cost | USD Equivalent |
|------|--------------|----------------|
| Basic skin pack | 160–310 coins | $1–2 |
| Character creator item | 100–500 coins | $0.65–3 |
| Map/adventure | 660–1,340 coins | $4–8 |
| Large texture pack | 1,340 coins | $8 |

For most players, 500 free Minecoins/month from Microsoft Rewards is enough to grab one skin pack or a smaller piece of Marketplace content each month.

## Free Alternatives to Buying Minecoins

Before spending Minecoins, know what's available for free:

**Java Edition skins** — If you have Java Edition, the skin system is completely free (upload any PNG image). Thousands of free community skins exist on sites like NameMC.

**Bedrock free skins** — Minecraft regularly adds free Character Creator items accessible without Minecoins. Check the Character Creator dressing room for free items.

**Community-made content** — While not on the official Marketplace, community resource packs and maps (downloaded manually) are free and often high quality.
