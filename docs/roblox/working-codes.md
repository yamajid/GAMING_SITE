## How Roblox Promo Codes Work

Roblox promo codes are released by Roblox Corporation and their brand partners. They award **free cosmetic items** (hats, accessories, pets) — not Robux directly. However, owning rare promotional items has value in the trading economy.

**Where codes come from:**
- Official Roblox Twitter/X announcements
- Brand partnerships (Target, Walmart, Amazon)
- Roblox events like Bloxy Awards
- YouTube creator promotions

## What Codes Don't Give You

Promo codes give **free items, not free Robux**. Any site claiming a promo code gives "1,000 free Robux" is lying. The only legitimate way to earn Robux is through [Roblox's own programs](/docs/roblox/earn-free-robux-2026).
