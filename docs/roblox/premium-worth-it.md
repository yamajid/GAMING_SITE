# Is Roblox Premium Worth It in 2026?

**Short answer:** Yes — if you play Roblox at least 3–4 times per week and spend Robux regularly. If you play casually or never buy cosmetics, skip it.

## The Three Roblox Premium Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Price | Monthly Robux | Cost per Robux |
|------|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| Premium 450 | $4.99/month | 450 Robux | $0.011/R |
| Premium 1000 | $9.99/month | 1,000 Robux | $0.010/R |
| Premium 2200 | $19.99/month | 2,200 Robux | $0.009/R |

**Buying Robux without Premium:** ~$0.0125 per Robux (800 Robux for $9.99)

The **Premium 1000 tier is the best value** for most players — you get 1,000 Robux for $9.99 vs only 800 Robux for the same price without Premium. That's a 25% bonus just for subscribing.

## What Else Do You Get With Premium?

Beyond the Robux stipend, Premium unlocks several exclusive features:

**Trading privileges** — Non-Premium players cannot trade limited items at all. If you want to participate in Roblox's item economy (buying/selling limited hats, collectibles), Premium is mandatory.

**Higher revenue share** — When you sell game passes or developer products, Premium members keep a larger percentage of each sale. This matters a lot if you're a game creator.

**Premium-only items** — The Roblox avatar shop regularly lists exclusive items only Premium subscribers can buy.

**Premium-gated games** — A growing number of community games restrict entry to Premium members only, giving you access to more content.

**DevEx benefits** — If you're a developer cashing out Robux into real money via Developer Exchange, Premium improves your eligibility and rate.

## When Premium Is NOT Worth It

Premium makes no sense if:
- You play fewer than 2–3 times per week
- You never spend Robux on in-game items or cosmetics
- You're under 13 and parents won't set up a recurring subscription
- Your only goal is earning free Robux — Premium requires payment and is not a free method

## Premium vs Buying Robux: Which Is Better?

**Choose Premium if:** You spend Robux every month consistently and want trading access and exclusive items.

**Buy Robux directly if:** You only need Robux occasionally (once or twice a year) and don't care about trading.

**The hidden value:** The trading privilege alone is worth Premium for collectors. Limited items regularly sell for thousands of Robux, and non-Premium players are completely locked out of that market.

## Does Premium Help You Earn More Robux?

Yes, in two ways:

1. **Selling items**: Premium members earn a higher revenue share on clothing and game passes they sell. A non-Premium creator earns 30% of each clothing sale; a Premium creator earns 70%.

2. **Trading**: Access to the limited item market means you can buy underpriced items and resell at profit — a common strategy for experienced Roblox traders.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I cancel Premium anytime?**
Yes. Roblox Premium is a monthly subscription with no lock-in. Cancel anytime and you keep your benefits until the end of the billing period.

**Does Premium give me Robux every month automatically?**
Yes. Your stipend is deposited on the same day each month automatically. You don't need to do anything.

**Is Premium available in all countries?**
Premium is available in most countries but pricing varies by region. Check roblox.com for your local price.

**Can kids under 13 get Premium?**
Yes, with parental consent. A parent or guardian needs to set up the payment method and approve the subscription.
