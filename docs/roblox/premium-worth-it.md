# Is Roblox Premium Worth It in 2026?

**Short answer:** Yes — if you play Roblox at least 3–4 times per week and spend Robux regularly.

## The Three Roblox Premium Tiers

| Tier | Monthly Price | Monthly Robux | Cost per Robux |
|------|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| Premium 450 | $4.99/month | 450 Robux | $0.011/R |
| Premium 1000 | $9.99/month | 1,000 Robux | $0.010/R |
| Premium 2200 | $19.99/month | 2,200 Robux | $0.009/R |

**Buying Robux without Premium:** ~$0.0125 per Robux (e.g. $9.99 for 800 Robux)

The 1000 Robux tier is almost always the best value — you get 1,000 Robux for $9.99 vs. 800 Robux for the same price without Premium.

## What Else Do You Get With Premium?

Beyond the Robux stipend, Premium members get:

- **Trading privileges** — Trade items with other players (non-Premium can't trade)
- **Robux from selling** — Sell items and game passes and keep a larger cut
- **Premium-only items** — Exclusive cosmetics in the Roblox avatar shop
- **Premium-gated games** — Some community games require Premium to enter
- **Better DevEx rate** — If you cash out Robux as a developer, Premium improves rates

## When Premium is NOT Worth It

- You only play casually once a month
- You never spend Robux on in-game purchases
- You're under 13 and can't set up recurring payments
- You're trying to "earn" Robux without spending — Premium requires payment and is not a free method

## The Real Math: 12-Month Comparison

If you subscribe to Premium 1000 for a full year:
- You pay: $119.88
- You receive: 12,000 Robux
- Buying same Robux directly: ~$150
- **You save: ~$30/year** plus all the bonus features

For active players, Premium pays for itself.

## Our Verdict

**Get Premium 1000 if:** You spend Robux on games, cosmetics, or avatar items at least twice a month.

**Skip Premium if:** You're trying to earn Robux for free — this guide isn't for you. Instead see our [free Robux methods guide](/docs/roblox/earn-free-robux-2026).
