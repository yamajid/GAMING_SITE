## Where PUBG Mobile Codes Come From

Unlike Roblox which releases codes weekly, PUBG Mobile codes are rarer and tied to:

**Official PUBG Mobile social media** — Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram. Follow @PUBGMOBILE for announcements.

**Esports events** — PMGC (Global Championship), PMPL (Pro League) events often include fan codes redeemable during broadcasts.

**Content creator partnerships** — PUBG Mobile partners with specific creators who share exclusive codes during streams.

**Anniversary and seasonal events** — Major celebrations (5th anniversary, etc.) reliably come with multiple codes.
