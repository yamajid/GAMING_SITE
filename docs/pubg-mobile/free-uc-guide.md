# How to Get Free UC in PUBG Mobile 2026

UC (Unknown Cash) is PUBG Mobile's premium currency — used for Royal Pass, crates, and outfit purchases. While truly free UC is limited, there are legitimate ways to earn it without spending real money.

:::warning Reality check
PUBG Mobile is more restrictive with free UC than games like Roblox or Genshin Impact. Most "free UC" methods involve third-party reward apps or specific promotions rather than in-game farming. Adjust your expectations accordingly.
:::

## Method 1: PUBG Mobile Events and Missions

PUBG Mobile regularly runs limited-time events that reward UC directly or give vouchers that reduce UC costs:

**Royale Pass missions** — Each season's free Royale Pass track includes UC rewards at specific tiers. You don't need to buy the pass to claim these.

**Anniversary and seasonal events** — Major PUBG Mobile celebrations (anniversary, Chinese New Year, etc.) typically include free UC in their reward tracks.

**Refer-a-friend promotions** — PUBG Mobile has occasionally run referral programs where both you and a new player earn UC. Check the events tab for current availability.

## Method 2: Google Opinion Rewards (Android)

This is the most reliable ongoing source of free Google Play credits that can be converted to UC purchases.

**How it works:**
1. Install Google Opinion Rewards from the Google Play Store
2. Answer short surveys (30–90 seconds) when they appear
3. Earn $0.10–$1.00 in Google Play credit per survey
4. Use that credit to buy UC in PUBG Mobile

**Expected earnings:** $3–15/month depending on your location and survey frequency. At PUBG Mobile's UC rates, this translates to 60–300 UC/month.

This is completely legitimate, safe, and officially supported by Google.

## Method 3: Microsoft Rewards (Xbox / PC Players)

If you play PUBG Mobile via an emulator on PC or through Xbox Game Pass, Microsoft Rewards points can be converted to Microsoft Store credit, which can be used for UC purchases.

**Earning Microsoft Rewards points:**
- Daily Bing searches
- Microsoft Edge usage bonuses
- Xbox Game Pass quests
- Monthly challenges

Heavy users earn $5–10/month in Microsoft credits — enough for 200–400 UC.

## Method 4: Watch Tournaments and Streams

PUBG Mobile regularly runs "watch and earn" promotions during official esports events (PMGC, PMPL). Linking your account and watching official streams for a set duration rewards UC or exclusive items.

Check the PUBG Mobile official app during major tournaments for current drop campaigns.

## Method 5: Midasbuy Promotions and Top-Up Bonuses

[Midasbuy](https://www.midasbuy.com) is PUBG Mobile's official top-up partner. They regularly run promotions offering bonus UC (10–50% extra) on purchases during special periods.

While this requires spending money, it's a way to maximize UC value when you do choose to spend.

## Royal Pass: Is the Free Version Worth It?

Every PUBG Mobile season includes both a free and paid Royale Pass. The free tier rewards are worth claiming even without paying:

- **Free RP missions** — earn BP, Avatar Frames, and occasional UC vouchers
- **Free tier cosmetics** — each season's free track includes outfits and accessories
- **RP point accumulation** — free missions contribute toward the paid tiers, so if you decide to upgrade mid-season, you start with progress

## What to Spend Free UC On

Since UC from free methods is limited, spend it strategically:

**Best value:** Upgrade Royal Pass early in the season — you'll earn back UC through the paid reward track, partially offsetting the cost.

**Avoid:** Crates and luck-based spins. The expected cost for rare items from crates is extremely high.
