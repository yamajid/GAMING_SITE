# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

Gaming Coins Hub ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at farmcoins.netlify.app. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

## Information We Collect

**Analytics data (automatically collected):**
When you visit our site, we collect standard analytics data through Google Analytics 4. This includes:
- Pages you visit and time spent on each page
- General geographic region (country/city level, not precise location)
- Browser type and operating system
- How you found our site (search engine, direct link, social media, etc.)
- Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

All analytics data is anonymized — IP addresses are truncated before storage and we cannot identify individual users from this data.

**No account required:**
We do not require registration or account creation. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personal details unless you voluntarily submit them (for example, through our newsletter signup form).

**Newsletter signup (optional):**
If you choose to sign up for our weekly codes newsletter, we collect your email address solely for the purpose of sending you the newsletter. We do not sell, share, or use this address for any other purpose. You can unsubscribe at any time.

## How We Use Your Information

We use analytics data to:
- Understand which guides are most useful to improve our content quality
- Monitor site performance and identify broken pages or issues
- Identify traffic sources to understand how players find us
- Make informed decisions about which games and topics to cover next

We do not:
- Sell your data to any third party
- Use your data for advertising targeting
- Share your information with game developers or publishers
- Build individual user profiles

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies:

**Analytics cookies** — Set by Google Analytics to measure aggregate traffic patterns. These cookies do not track you across other websites and contain no personally identifiable information.

**Preference cookies** — Docusaurus (our website platform) stores your light/dark mode preference locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.

You can disable cookies entirely in your browser settings without affecting your ability to read our content. With cookies disabled, we simply receive less accurate aggregate traffic data.

## Third-Party Links

Our guides contain links to external sites including roblox.com, epicgames.com, and other game developer sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

## Data Retention

Google Analytics retains data for 14 months by default under our account settings. We do not retain any other user data beyond what Google Analytics collects automatically.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics tracking

To exercise these rights or ask questions about this policy, contact us via [Twitter/X @GamingCoinsHub](https://twitter.com/GamingCoinsHub).

## Children's Privacy

Our content is suitable for all ages, including children who play games like Roblox. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our analytics data is fully anonymized and does not enable identification of any individual user, including minors.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally as our practices or legal requirements change. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
