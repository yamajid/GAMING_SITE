# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 2026*

Gaming Coins Hub ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at farmcoins.netlify.app. This page explains how we handle your data.

## Information We Collect

**Automatically collected data:** When you visit our site, we collect standard analytics data including pages visited, time on page, browser type, and approximate geographic region. This is collected via Google Analytics 4 and is anonymized (IP addresses are truncated).

**No account required:** We do not require registration. We do not collect names, emails, or personal details unless you voluntarily contact us.

## How We Use Information

- Understand which guides are most useful to improve content
- Monitor site performance and fix broken pages
- Identify traffic sources to grow the site

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except as required by law.

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies — primarily Google Analytics cookies for aggregate traffic measurement. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

## Third-Party Links

Our guides may contain links to external sites (Roblox, Epic Games, etc.). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

## Children's Privacy

Our site is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

## Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via our [Twitter/X account](https://twitter.com/GamingCoinsHub).
