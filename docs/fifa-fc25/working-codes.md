## Transfer Market Tips for Beginners

The Transfer Market is intimidating at first but becomes the most powerful free coin source with practice. Three beginner-friendly strategies:

**1. Buy Low on Sunday nights** — Player prices drop on Sunday evenings (EU time) as Weekend League ends and people sell their rewards. Buy cards at weekend lows, list them Monday morning at full price.

**2. SBC deadline flipping** — When EA announces an SBC requirement (e.g., "84-rated squad"), the specific required cards spike in price. Buy before the announcement, sell during the price peak.

**3. Inform card anticipation** — EA releases "Inform" (Team of the Week) cards every Wednesday. In-form versions of popular players spike in value the moment EA announces them. Buy the base card Tuesday night, sell the inform Wednesday after the announcement.

All three strategies require patience and attention to market timing — but generate coins without spending real money.
