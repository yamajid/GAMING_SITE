# Free FC Points and Coins in EA FC 25 — 2026 Guide

EA FC 25 Ultimate Team has two currencies: **FC Points** (premium, real money) and **Coins** (earned in-game). This guide focuses on maximizing your free coins and minimizing or eliminating the need to spend real money.

:::info FC Points vs Coins
FC Points can only be purchased with real money. This guide covers earning **Coins** for free through gameplay, and legitimate strategies to maximize your squad without spending FC Points.
:::

## Earning Coins Through Gameplay

### Squad Battles — 10,000–80,000+ Coins/Week
Squad Battles is the single best source of free coins in Ultimate Team. Play against AI squads, earn points, and claim weekly rewards based on your final rank.

| Rank | Coins (approx) | Packs |
|------|---------------|-------|
| Elite 1 | 70,000+ | Gold + Premium |
| Elite 3 | 50,000+ | Gold packs |
| Gold 1 | 30,000 | Gold packs |
| Silver 1 | 15,000 | Silver packs |

Reaching Gold 1 requires roughly 12–15 matches per week on Semi-Pro difficulty.

### Division Rivals — Weekly Rewards
Playing Division Rivals earns Weekly Objectives and a rank-based reward at week's end. Aim for Division 5 or higher to receive meaningful coin rewards.

### Daily Objectives — 500–2,000 Coins/Day
Complete daily objectives in the Objectives tab. Each takes 5–10 minutes and rewards coins, packs, or player picks.

## Trading on the Transfer Market (Best Long-Term Method)

The FUT Transfer Market is the most powerful tool for free-to-play players. Understanding the market can generate more coins than gameplay alone.

### Bronze Pack Method (BPM)
The simplest beginner trading strategy:
1. Buy bronze packs (400–750 coins each)
2. Open them and list all consumables (fitness cards, contracts) on the market at a markup
3. Quick-sell the rest
4. Net profit: 200–500 coins per pack over time

### Mass Bidding
1. Pick a player card at a specific price range
2. Bid on 20–30 of them near expiry at minimum bid
3. Win some below market value
4. Relist at market price for profit

This requires patience but generates consistent profit with zero risk once you're experienced.

### SBC Fodder Flipping
Squad Building Challenges consume specific player cards. When new SBCs launch, demand spikes for the required cards. Buy those players before the SBC drops and sell when demand peaks.

## Squad Building Challenges (SBCs) for Free Packs

SBCs are puzzles where you submit player cards meeting certain criteria in exchange for packs and rewards. The rewards often exceed the coin cost of the cards submitted.

**Repeatable SBCs** — Some SBCs reset daily or weekly and consistently return profit when completed with cheap fodder.

**Lightning Rounds** — Time-limited SBCs with exceptional rewards. Complete these immediately when they appear.

## Objectives and Milestones

The Objectives system gives free packs, coins, and player items for completing in-game challenges. These are entirely free and just require playing the game.

**Season objectives** — Each season has a reward track. Complete objectives to unlock a high-rated player for free — often worth 50,000–200,000 coins on the market.

**Milestone objectives** — Permanent challenges that reward significant packs and coins when completed.

## FC Points: Are They Worth It?

FC Points are only worth buying for specific high-value situations:

- **Launch week pack openings** — Card prices are at their seasonal peak during launch week; pack value is highest then
- **Team of the Year (TOTY)** — The best promo of the year; packs have highest expected value

For free-to-play players, the answer is almost always: **no**. The Transfer Market and objectives provide enough coins to build a competitive squad in every division without spending real money.
