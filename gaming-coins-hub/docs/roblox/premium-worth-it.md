**Premium ($4.99/month):**
- Monthly stipend: 450 Robux
- Robux value: 1 USD = ~200 Robux
- Stipend value: 450 ÷ 200 = **$2.25**
- **Breakeven:** NO (stipend worth $2.25, costs $4.99)

**Premium Plus ($9.99/month):**

## ⚡ FAQ

**Q: Does the monthly Robux show up automatically?**  
A: Yes. Your Premium Robux stipend deposits on the same date each month automatically.

**Q: Can I cancel Premium anytime?**  
A: Yes. No lock-in period. Cancel instantly from Settings → Billing.

**Q: Do I lose Robux if I cancel Premium?**  
A: No. You keep all Robux earned. Premium just stops giving new stipends.

**Q: Is Premium better value than buying Robux directly?**  
A: As Robux value: No. But as benefit package: Slightly yes (10-15% savings for heavy buyers).

**Q: Can I use Premium Robux to buy Premium again?**  
A: No. Premium costs real USD only, can't pay with Robux.
