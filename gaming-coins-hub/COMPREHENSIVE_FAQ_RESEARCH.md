### Beginner Questions
**Q: What are Robux?**
- A: Virtual currency in Roblox used to buy cosmetics, game passes, premium features, and limited items. 1 Robux = 1 in-game currency unit.

**Q: What is the difference between Robux and Premium?**
- A: Robux = currency you purchase/earn. Premium = monthly subscription (~$9.99) giving daily Robux stipend + 10% bonus on purchases.

**Q: How much does Roblox Premium cost?**
- A: $4.99/month (120 Robux daily stipend) or $9.99/month (100 Robux daily stipend + 10% bonus on all purchases) or $19.99/month.

**Q: Are there any legitimate ways to get free Robux?**
- A: Yes - promo codes, Premium stipend, free Robux earned from creator program, passing Builders Club stipend. NO generators that promise free unlimited Robux work.

**Q: Why do Robux generators claim to work?**
- A: They're scams designed to steal account credentials or install malware. Roblox has NO official free Robux generator.

### Earning & Money Questions
**Q: Can you make money from Roblox games?**
- A: Yes, through DevEx (Developer Exchange) - convert 100,000 Robux = $350 USD (at minimum level). Only available if you meet criteria: 13+ years old, 100K+ Robux earned from games, phone verification.

**Q: How much can you realistically earn?**
- A: 100,000 Robux = ~$350 USD. Top developers earn $10K-$100K+ monthly from popular games. Average developers earn $50-$500 monthly.

**Q: How much do popular Robux packages cost?**
- A: 400 Robux = $4.99 | 800 Robux = $9.99 | 1700 Robux = $19.99 | 4500 Robux = $49.99

**Q: What percentage cut does Roblox take?**
- A: Roblox takes 30% commission on all in-game purchases. Developers keep 70%.

### Promo Codes & Collections Questions
**Q: Do promo codes give real Robux?**
- A: YES - limited promo codes (usually event-specific) give 100-550 FREE Robux directly to your account.

**Q: Where do I find new promo codes?**
- A: Official Roblox social media (Twitter @Roblox), YouTube channel reveals, seasonal events, official blog, and community sites like promocodes.roblox.com (if still active).

**Q: How often do new promo codes release?**
- A: 1-2 codes per month typically during events, seasonal updates, or special promotions. They expire in 1-3 months.

**Q: Which promo codes work right now (March 2026)?**
- A: Check our updated "Working Codes" guide - we update weekly as new codes release and old ones expire.

**Q: Can promo codes be used multiple times?**
- A: NO - each account can only redeem each code ONCE. Ever.

**Q: What happens if you try to redeem expired codes?**

### Premium & Pass Questions
**Q: Is Roblox Premium worth the money?**
- A: YES if you: Play regularly (5+ hours/week), want daily Robux, enjoy 10% marketplace discounts. NO if you're casual/F2P only.

**Q: What benefits do you get with Premium?**
- A: Daily Robux stipend (120-100 depending on tier), 10% bonus on Robux purchases, exclusive items, monthly game passes.

**Q: What's a Game Pass?**
- A: One-time purchase per game (created by developers, usually 10-500 Robux) giving permanent perks like double XP, exclusive areas, cosmetics.

**Q: Can you get Game Passes for free?**
- A: NO - Game Passes must be purchased with Robux. Some games occasionally give away Game Pass codes.

### Scam & Safety Questions
**Q: Is this Robux generator website safe?**
- A: NO. All "free Robux" generators, hacks, and surveys are 100% scams. They steal accounts or install malware.

**Q: What's the #1 Robux scam?**
- A: "Survey sites offering free Robux" - you complete surveys, never get Robux, and your personal data is sold.

**Q: What happens if I use a Robux generator?**
- A: Your account gets hacked, password stolen, Robux stolen, and account permanently banned by Roblox.

**Q: How do I report a Roblox scam?**
- A: Report to Roblox trust & safety: right-click player → "Report" → select violation type. Or report website to scamwarning.org.

**Q: Is Roblox affiliate links safe?**
- A: Use only links from VERIFIED creators (official partners with badges). Unknown links likely phishing scams.

### Creator & Income Questions
**Q: How do I start earning Robux as a creator?**
- A: Create game(s), earn Robux from players, reach 100K Robux minimum, enable DevEx, exchange for real money.

**Q: What's the DevEx exchange rate?**
