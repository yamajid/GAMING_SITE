How to Get Free Diamonds in Mobile Legends 2026

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Best Method

Monthly Pass + Event Farming = 500-1000 free diamonds/month. Takes ~1-2 hours daily gameplay.

✅ 5 Legitimate Ways to Get Free Diamonds​

1. Monthly Pass & Weekly Bonus (Most consistent)​

  • Diamonds/month: 300-500
  • How:
    1. Buy weekly diamond pass ($1.99/week or battle pass)
    2. Complete daily tasks
    3. Get diamond bonus on Day 7
  • ROI: $1.99 = 150 diamonds usually
  • Best for: Light spenders

2. Major Events (Seasonal)​

  • Diamonds: 200-500 per event
  • Frequency: 3-4 major events per month
  • How: Participate in limited-time missions
  • Examples:
    • Lunar New Year event
    • Anniversary event
    • Collaboration events
  • Reality: Very variable rewards

3. Achievement Rewards (One-time)​

  • Diamonds: 50-100 per achievement
  • How: Complete in-game achievements
  • Total potential: 2,000+ diamonds (all achievements)
  • Time to complete: 1-3 months of regular play
  • Best for: Long-term players

4. Login Streaks & Rewards (Daily)​

  • Diamonds: 5-20/day
  • How: Log in daily, don't miss streaks
  • Monthly total: 150-600 diamonds
  • Best for: Committed players
  • Reality: Easy but small amounts

5. Redeem Codes (Rare)​

  • Diamonds: Usually 0 (skins/items instead)
  • How: Search "Mobile Legends redeem codes" monthly
  • Frequency: 2-5 codes per month
  • Where: Official website, social media
  • Reality: Most codes don't give diamonds

📊 Monthly Earnings Breakdown​

Casual Player (1-2 hrs/day)​

  • Daily login: 150 diamonds
  • Monthly event: 200 diamonds
  • Pass bonus: 0 diamonds
  • Total: ~350/month

Regular Player (3-5 hrs/day)​

  • Daily login: 300 diamonds
  • Monthly events: 300 diamonds
  • Pass bonuses: 300 diamonds
  • Achievements: 50 diamonds
  • Total: ~950/month

Hardcore Player (5+ hrs/day)​

  • Daily login: 600 diamonds
  • Monthly events: 500 diamonds
  • Pass bonuses: 500 diamonds
  • Achievements: 100 diamonds
  • Total: ~1,700/month

🎯 Best Strategy for 2026​

Month 1: Build Foundation​

  • Start daily login streak
  • Complete easy achievements
  • Track all monthly events
  • Join team for event bonuses
  • Expected: 300-400 diamonds

Month 2-3: Maximize Events​

  • Never miss a login day
  • Coordinate event farming with team
  • Complete battle pass missions
  • Stock up diamonds
  • Expected: 800-1,200 diamonds

Month 4+: Sustain Income​

  • Maintain 100+ day login streak
  • Farm seasonal events efficiently
  • Buy occasional pass ($2/week)
  • Save for hero purchases
  • Expected: 1,000+ diamonds/month

⚡ FAQ​

Q: Is there really a way to get unlimited diamonds?
A: No. Limit is determined by your play activity + events.

Q: How often do events give diamonds?
A: Usually 3-4 major events/month = 200-500 diamonds/month.

Q: Can I earn diamonds just from matches?
A: No. Only from tasks, events, and login streaks.

Q: What's the max diamonds I can earn in a month?
A: Realistically 1,500-2,000 if you play 5+ hours daily and complete everything.

A: Yes, but diamonds are rare. Usually cosmetics/BP items.
