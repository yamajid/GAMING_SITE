💡 Pro Tips​

1. Know League Requirements

  • Gold: Need ~800 trophies
  • Crystal: Need ~1,200 trophies
  • Titan: Need ~2,000 trophies
  • Champions: Need ~2,500 trophies

2. Farm with Good Army

  • Use cheap armies for trophies
  • Focus on getting 70% stars quickly
  • Don't waste resources on dead-end attacks

3. Join Active Clan for Wars

  • War wins give 50 gems eventually
  • War participation is mandatory
  • Multiple people = faster war frequency

4. Build Destroyer Army

  • Barbarians + Archers combo
  • Cheap to train
  • Good for farming + achievement grinding
  • Perfect for "Total Destruction" achievement

5. Spread Gameplay

  • Don't grind one achievement
  • Mix league climbing + resource collection
  • Prevents burnout
  • Helps overall base development

⚡ FAQ​

Q: Which achievements give the most gems?
A: League achievements (Gold/Crystal/Titan/Champions) = 50 gems each. Total 200 gems from all 4.

Q: How long does it take to get 450 gems?
A: Casual player: 3-6 months. Active player: 1-2 months.

Q: Can I earn gems back after reaching an achievement?
A: No. Achievements = one-time only. After 450 gems, you stop earning from achievements.

Q: Are war achievements worth the grind?
A: Only if you already participate in wars for fun. Otherwise skip (slow + requires clan).

Q: Should I rush to higher leagues?
A: Yes. League progression is fastest gems. Reach Crystal League first, then worry about others.
